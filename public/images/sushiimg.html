All of our Sushi is prepared by experienced Sushi chefs, using fresh natural ingredients. Our vegetables, fish, and fruit are carefully selected to ensure a high quality food that will delight your tastebuds.

Wide selection of sushi

We have a wide range of dishes available, which means we have all your favourite types of Sushi, and probably also some you haven’t tried before. We also have all the classic condiments that you would expect to find such as wasabi, ginger and soy sauce.

The fresh and healthy choice

Sushi is not only delicious, but it’s good for you too. Made with low-fat, high-protein ingredients, Sushi is great for health-conscious people. It’s full of healthy vitamins and minerals, and also contains natural fish oils.
